About
What is METRONET?

METRONET is a creative-mode Minecraft server built entirely around trains and railway infrastructure. Whether you want to construct grand central stations, lay intercity rail lines, or design complex metro systems — this is the place to do it.

We run a custom modpack that brings authentic train mechanics to Minecraft, with a friendly and passionate community behind it.

The map is open for collaboration — jump in and start building.
